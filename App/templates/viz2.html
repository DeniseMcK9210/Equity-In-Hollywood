Women of Their Word

2009
2019
Analysis

The third guideline of the Bechdel-Wallace Test prompted us to ask . . .

“If a conversation between two women was not about a man, what was it about?”

Reviewer comments on BechdelTest.com for movies passing the Bechdel Test were analyzed. These movies have women that converse about meatloaf, life, death, and their childhoods. Some conversations were about saving the world or their careers. The most overwhelming evidence from the sample set, however, proved that nail polish and perfume led the topic list!
